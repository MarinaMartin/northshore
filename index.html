About Us

Northshore Rock Products is a premier producer and provider of construction aggregates, decorative rock products, and other stone and naturally occurring earthen materials.

With a unique infrastructure of strategically-located truck and rail-served quarries containing over two billion tons of aggregate reserves, Northshore can produce and deliver many of the primary aggregate products used in the construction, transportation, oil and gas, industrial and agricultural industries.

Products mined and produced from Northshores quarry reserves include custom sizes, types, and colors of crushed and screened quartz, limestone, dolomite and cobble stone, a large variety of landscape and decorative rock, a variety of natural occurring and crushed sands, and a unique reserve of high-grade diatomite and other earthen materials.

Product applications range from materials used in building railways and roadways, waterways and pipelines, large and small civil and commercial construction, and various oil, gas, and industrial classification and screening applications.
